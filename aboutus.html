At Pott. Handmade Ceramics & Pottery Studio

Once upon a time...

Nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Vietnam, a pottery studio emerged with a burning passion for the art of pottery. This studio, aptly named Pott. Handmade Ceramics & Pottery Studio, embarked on a remarkable journey, becoming a leading force in the pottery industry with over 10 years of establishment and development.

From its humble beginnings, Pott. Handmade Ceramics & Pottery Studio set out to preserve the rich cultural heritage of Vietnam's pottery craftsmanship. The founders, a group of skilled artisans, recognized the importance of carrying forward traditional techniques while embracing modern aesthetics. Their commitment to excellence and unwavering dedication to their craft propelled the studio to new heights.

VISION

To become Vietnam's most meaningful handmade pottery brand, fostering a deep connection between people, art, and cultural heritage.

QUALITY

Our unwavering commitment to quality is a testament to our passion for pottery and our desire to create enduring pieces that bring joy and beauty into people's lives.
